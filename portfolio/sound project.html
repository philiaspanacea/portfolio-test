Sound Design

Audio Replacement

Overview

For this project, I replaced about a minute of music and sound effects from 'The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom' trailer. This was a tedious process that required syncing audio to video—achieved using DaVinci Resolve—finding the right audio, and gauging believability that this could be the actual trailer. Going into this project, I hadn't watched the original trailer with sound. I challenged myself by referencing the video originally muted. When I completed the project and listened to the trailer, I noticed that I made similar decisions as the Nintendo team, so I was prouder of the outcome than before. While working on this project, I drew from my memory of playing the game itself and I think that, along with intuitive feeling, helps in the process of finding the right sound effects and music.

Clip

This trailer shows the character Link facing hardships before he embarks on a journey across this world. The technology he now has access to allows him to manipulate time, use portals, and harness fire.
