IU Rebrand Kit

2025

"The Decided Light"

2025

After a few sketch iterations, “The Decided Light” was created with an achromatic palette in Adobe Illustrator, then with a chromatic palette in Adobe Photoshop. I took inspiration from the use of achromatic and chromatic value contrast and semi-abstraction in the work of artists Okuda and Brad Eastman.

"King's Corners" Demo

2025

This video demonstrates how I transformed simple graphic design in a Canva slide deck into an animation with meaning. Essentially, each slide during the game acted as a keyframe. Through the right timing, static cards came to life and told the story I wanted to convey of a competitive game of cards.

Radial Design

2025

This project is a radial design inspired by the radial symmetry in Gothic Rose windows. After some research, I felt drawn to take inspiration from sea life to create an environment through the design. Nature and 'radial' go hand-in-hand, so I wanted my design to feel organic and natural.

"Animals in Art"

2025

This project is a Canva slide deck detailing the design choices of various pieces of art in a museum exhibit. I wanted to find a unique way of designing each page that felt like an extension of the artwork that is being presented on said page, such as using the same color palette and using different elements to mimic the ones in the pieces.
